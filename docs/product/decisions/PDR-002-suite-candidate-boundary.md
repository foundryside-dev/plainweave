# PDR-002: Treat Beta As Candidate, Not Admission

## Status

Accepted for beta candidate.

## Context

Plainweave depends on Loomweave identity and Legis policy boundaries. Formal
suite admission and outward-facing release steps affect the larger suite.

## Decision

Engineering may implement local beta capability and dogfood it. Formal suite
membership, final product name, hub roster edits, PyPI/public release, and
sibling obligations remain owner-gated.

## Reversal Trigger

Revisit when the owner explicitly approves suite admission or publication work.
